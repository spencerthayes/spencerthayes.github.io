Pac-man Berries

When Pac-man eats a berry he can eat ghosts. Berries also give him other abilities.



Regular

When Pac-man eats the regular berry, he can fly.

Balloon Berry

When Pac-man eats a balloon berry, he blows up like a balloon.

Fire Berry

When Pac-man eats the fire berry he can protect himself from the pacinator and shoot fireballs out of his mouth.


Minty Ice Berry

When pac-man eats a minty ice berry he can eat a fire ghost without getting a heartburn and he can freeze things.


Kung Fu Berry

When pac-man eats a kung fu berry he can do kung fu without even knowing it.


Pac-zilla Berry

Immense increase in size and strength for 10 minutes or less.


Wind Berry

if pac-man eats it and breathe out he’lblow away.


Titanium Berry

Can use magnet and drill abilities.
